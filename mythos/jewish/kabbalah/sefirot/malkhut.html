Key Concepts

Malkhut is identified with the Shekhinahâ€”the Divine Presence that dwells within creation. Where Keter is the transcendent God beyond comprehension, the Shekhinah is the immanent God accessible within the world. She is the Mother in exile, the Presence in matter.
