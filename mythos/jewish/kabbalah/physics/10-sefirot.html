The Kabbalistic 10

The Ten Sefirot

In Kabbalah, the Ten Sefirot are the divine emanations through which the infinite Ein Sof (the Limitless One) manifests reality. They form the Tree of Life, a structural map of how unity differentiates into multiplicity.

The Ten Sefirot:
  1. Keter (Crown) - Will, highest unity
  2. Chokmah (Wisdom) - Primordial point, expansion
  3. Binah (Understanding) - Structure, contraction
  4. Chesed (Mercy) - Loving-kindness, expansion
  5. Gevurah (Severity) - Strength, restriction
  6. Tiferet (Beauty) - Harmony, balance
  7. Netzach (Victory) - Endurance, expansion
  8. Hod (Glory) - Splendor, contraction
  9. Yesod (Foundation) - Connection, transmission
  10. Malkhut (Kingdom) - Physical manifestation

Three Pillars

The Sefirot arrange into three pillars:

  • Right Pillar (Mercy) - Chokmah, Chesed, Netzach - Expansive forces
  • Left Pillar (Severity) - Binah, Gevurah, Hod - Restrictive forces
  • Middle Pillar (Balance) - Keter, Tiferet, Yesod, Malkhut - Equilibrium
