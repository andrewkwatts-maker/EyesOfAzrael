🔯 Kabbalah - The Mystical Core

Kabbalah ("receiving") is the ancient mystical tradition of Judaism, revealing the hidden dimensions of Torah and the structure of reality itself. It encompasses the Tree of Life, the divine emanations, and the pathways of spiritual ascent.

📿 The 10 Sefirot

The ten divine emanations through which Ein Sof (the Infinite) reveals itself and creates reality. Each Sefirah represents a different aspect of divine consciousness and creative power.

1. Keter (Crown) 2. Chokmah (Wisdom) 3. Binah (Understanding) 4. Chesed (Mercy) 5. Gevurah (Severity) 6. Tiferet (Beauty) 7. Netzach (Victory) 8. Hod (Splendor) 9. Yesod (Foundation) 10. Malkhut (Kingdom)

🌍 The 4 Worlds

The four levels of reality through which divine light descends from the Infinite to the material realm. Each world represents a different level of divine concealment and manifestation.

Atziluth (Emanation) Beriah (Creation) Yetzirah (Formation) Assiah (Action)

✡️ The 72 Names of God

The Shemhamphorasch - 72 three-letter divine names derived from Exodus 14:19-21. Each name carries unique powers and purposes, used for meditation, healing, and spiritual transformation.

1. והו (Vehu) 2. ילי (Yeli) 3. סיט (Sit) View All 72 →

⚡ The 288 Sparks

The complete system: 72 Names × 4 Worlds = 288 divine sparks. Each spark represents a unique manifestation of divine light across the four levels of reality, creating a comprehensive character and force compendium.

Vehu in Atziluth Vehu in Beriah View All 288 →

🌑 The Qlippot (Shadow Tree)

The shells or husks - the realm of divine sparks trapped in material form. The shadow counterpart to the Tree of Life, representing the forces of divine concealment and spiritual challenges.

👼 Angels & Demons

The celestial hierarchy and fallen beings. Angels as messengers and executors of divine will, demons as spiritual challenges and tests of faith.

📖 Kabbalistic Concepts

Core teachings including Tzimtzum (divine contraction), Shevirat HaKelim (breaking of vessels), Tikkun Olam (repairing the world), and the nature of divine light.

🪜 The Ascension Path

The 6-stage journey from novice to master Kabbalist, spanning 20-30 years of study, practice, and spiritual transformation.
