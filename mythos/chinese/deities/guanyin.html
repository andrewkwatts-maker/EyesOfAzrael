Guanyin

观音 / 観音 (Guān Yīn) - Goddess of Compassion and Mercy

Guanyin, also known as Kuan Yin or Avalokiteshvara, is the East Asian Bodhisattva of compassion and mercy. Originally a male deity in Indian Buddhism, Guanyin transformed into a beloved feminine figure in Chinese religious culture, embodying the ideal of infinite compassion who hears the cries of all suffering beings.
