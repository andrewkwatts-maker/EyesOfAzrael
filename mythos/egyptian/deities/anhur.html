Anhur (Onuris)

God of War and Hunting

Anhur is the warrior god and divine hunter, 'He Who Brings Back the Distant One.' Associated with bringing Hathor/Tefnut back from Nubia when she fled Egypt in rage. Depicted as bearded man with four feathers on head, holding spear or lance. Patron of the Egyptian army and royal hunting expeditions.
