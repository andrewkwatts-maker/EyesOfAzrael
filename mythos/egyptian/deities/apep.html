Apep (Apophis)

Serpent of Chaos, Enemy of Ra

Apep is the monstrous chaos serpent who attacks Ra's solar barque nightly during its journey through the Duat. Embodiment of isfet (chaos, disorder, darkness), eternally battling ma'at (order). Defeated nightly by Set and Ra's defenders, but never permanently destroyed. Apep's victory would mean the end of creation itself.
