Set (Seth, Sutekh)

God of Chaos, Desert, Storms, and Foreigners

Set is the god of chaos, the desert, storms, and disorder. Red-haired and fierce, Set murdered his brother Osiris but was defeated by Horus. Though often cast as villain, Set also defended Ra's solar barque against the chaos serpent Apep nightly. He represents necessary disorder, the wild desert, and the power of storms.
