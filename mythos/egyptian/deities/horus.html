Horus (Heru, Hor)

God of Kingship, Sky, and Vengeance

Horus is the falcon-headed god of the sky, kingship, and divine right to rule. As the son of Osiris and Isis, he avenged his father's murder by defeating Set and became the divine prototype of the pharaoh. The living king was considered Horus incarnate, while the dead king became Osiris.
