Geb (Seb, Keb)

God of the Earth

Geb is the earth god, depicted lying beneath his sister-consort Nut (sky). Father of Osiris, Isis, Set, and Nephthys. His laughter causes earthquakes, and vegetation grows from his body. As earth god, he judged disputes between Horus and Set, ultimately awarding rulership to Horus.
