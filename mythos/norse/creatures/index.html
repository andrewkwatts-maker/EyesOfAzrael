Fenrir

The Great Wolf

The monstrous wolf, son of Loki and the giantess Angrboda. Fenrir grew so large and fearsome that the gods bound him with the magical chain Gleipnir, but he will break free at Ragnarok and devour Odin himself.

Jormungandr

The World Serpent

The Midgard Serpent, another child of Loki. Odin threw Jormungandr into the ocean where the serpent grew so large it encircles the entire world and grasps its own tail.

Sleipnir

The Eight-Legged Horse

Odin's magnificent eight-legged steed. Born when Loki transformed into a mare to distract the giant's stallion Svadilfari. Sleipnir can travel between the worlds, fastest of all horses.

Abilities: Can ride through all Nine Realms
