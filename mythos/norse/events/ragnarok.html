The Twilight of the Gods

The prophesied end of the world - a cataclysmic battle between gods and monsters that will destroy and renew the cosmos.

See Ragnarok (Concept) for full details about this pivotal event in Norse cosmology.

Ragnarok represents the Norse understanding of cyclical time - destruction leads to renewal, death to rebirth. The gods fight knowing they will fall, embodying the heroic ideal of facing doom with courage.
