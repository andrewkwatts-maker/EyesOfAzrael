The Valkyries - Choosers of the Slain

Odin's warrior maidens who select fallen heroes from battlefields to bring to Valhalla. Divine shield-maidens who serve the Einherjar mead and decide the fates of warriors in combat.

The Shield-Maidens of Odin

The Valkyries ("choosers of the slain") are fierce warrior women who ride through battlefields on flying horses, selecting which warriors will die and which will live. Those they choose are brought to Valhalla to join Odin's army of Einherjar. In Valhalla, the Valkyries serve as cupbearers, bringing mead and meat to the heroes.
