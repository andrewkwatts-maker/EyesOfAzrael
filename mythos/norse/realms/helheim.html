Helheim - Realm of the Dead

The cold, misty realm ruled by Hel, where those who die of sickness, old age, or without glory go after death.

The Land of the Dead

Helheim is a cold, misty realm located in Niflheim, the world of ice and fog. It is not a place of punishment like later Christian Hell, but rather a gray, joyless existence. The dead who dwell there are shades, going through the motions of life without its warmth or vitality.
