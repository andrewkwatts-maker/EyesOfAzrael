Eir

Goddess of Healing

The goddess or valkyrie associated with healing and medicine. Eir possesses extraordinary medical knowledge and is invoked for healing wounds and curing illnesses. She is counted among the most skillful physicians in the Nine Realms.

Mythology

Eir is mentioned in the Prose Edda as one of Frigg's handmaidens and the best of physicians. Some sources suggest she may also be a valkyrie who tends to wounded warriors. She is associated with medicinal herbs and the healing arts, making her a patron of healers and physicians. Her name means "help" or "mercy" in Old Norse.
