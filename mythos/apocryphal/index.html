üìñ Primary Sources

  • 1 Enoch (Ethiopic Enoch) - The most complete Enochian text, detailing the Watchers and celestial journeys (3rd-1st century BCE)
  • 2 Enoch (Slavonic Enoch) - Account of Enoch's ascent through the heavens (1st century CE)
  • 3 Enoch (Hebrew Enoch) - Transformation of Enoch into the angel Metatron (5th-6th century CE)
  • Book of Jubilees - Retelling of Genesis with expanded angelology (2nd century BCE)
  • Book of Giants - Dead Sea Scroll fragments about the Nephilim (2nd-1st century BCE)
  • Testament of Solomon - King Solomon's mastery over demons (1st-3rd century CE)
  • Apocalypse of Abraham - Visions of creation and the end times (1st-2nd century CE)
