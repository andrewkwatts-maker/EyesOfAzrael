The Vertical Cosmos - Seven Heavens

Seventh Heaven - Araboth

Araboth - The Highest Heaven

The dwelling place of God, where the divine throne sits upon the Merkabah. Here are righteousness, justice, mercy, and the souls of the unborn. The Seraphim cry "Holy, Holy, Holy!"

Inhabitants: God, Seraphim, Ophanim, Archangels, Metatron

Sixth Heaven - Machon

Machon - The Foundation

Home of scholar angels who study Torah, astronomy, and sciences of creation. The heavenly archives storing records of all earthly events are kept here.

Inhabitants: Scholar Angels, Cherubim, Phoenixes, Keepers of Records

Fifth Heaven - Maon

Maon - The Residence

Home of the Ishim, angels of fire who eternally sing praises. The Watchers' leaders including Azazel are imprisoned here. Massive angelic armies await divine commands.

Inhabitants: Ishim (fiery angels), Angels of Destruction, Imprisoned Chiefs

Fourth Heaven - Zebul

Zebul - The Dwelling

Contains the heavenly Jerusalem and Holy Temple where Michael serves as High Priest. The Sun and Moon have thrones here, directed by Uriel and Gabriel.

Inhabitants: Michael, Temple Angels, Sun and Moon, Chariot Wheels

Third Heaven - Shehaqim

Shehaqim - The Clouds

Paradise (Eden) and Gehenna coexist here. The Tree of Life grows in the north where the righteous dwell, while fire and ice torture the wicked in the south.

Inhabitants: Righteous Souls, Tormented Wicked, Angels of Paradise, Phoenix

Second Heaven - Raquia

Raquia - The Firmament

Prison of the fallen Watchers who await final judgment. Here in darkness and chains, 200 rebellious angels suffer. Contains the treasury of celestial fire.

Inhabitants: Imprisoned Watchers, Angels of Luminaries, Spirits of Darkness

First Heaven - Vilon

Vilon - The Veil

The lowest heaven, containing storehouses of snow, hail, dew, and rain. Angels who govern weather and seasons dwell here. The Elders command the stars.

Inhabitants: Angels of Weather, Star-Elders, Clouds of Glory

Earth - Eretz

Earth - The Material World

The middle realm where humanity dwells. Created on the third day, it is the stage for spiritual warfare and the testing ground of souls.

Inhabitants: Humans, Animals, Plants, Spirits

Sheol / Gehenna

Sheol / Gehenna - The Underworld

The realm beneath earth where the dead await judgment. Paradise for the righteous, seven chambers of punishment for the wicked. Some are purified, others remain forever.

Locations: Paradise, Seven Chambers of Gehenna

The Abyss

The Abyss - Deepest Prison

Deep chambers where the Watchers are imprisoned in chains of darkness, awaiting final judgment. A place of weeping, torment, and eternal separation from the divine light.

Inhabitants: Bound Watchers, Fallen Angels, Demons
