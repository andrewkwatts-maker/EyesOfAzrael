Krishna

The Divine Cowherd

Krishna is the eighth avatar of Vishnu , the divine cowherd who revealed the Bhagavad Gita.

Archetype

Krishna embodies the Divine Child and Savior archetypes - the miraculous infant who escapes death, the playful youth, and the wise teacher who guides humanity.
