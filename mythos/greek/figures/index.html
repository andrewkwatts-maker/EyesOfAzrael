Important Figures of Greek Myth

Notable characters who play important roles in Greek mythology - judges, ferryman, and other significant figures who help maintain the cosmic order.

Underworld Figures

Charon

The ferryman of Hades who carries souls across the River Styx to the underworld. He requires payment of a coin (obol) placed in the mouth of the deceased.

The Three Judges

Three kings who became judges of the dead in the underworld, determining the fate of souls:

  • Minos - King of Crete, judge of the final vote
  • Rhadamanthys - Judge of Asian souls
  • Aeacus - Judge of European souls
