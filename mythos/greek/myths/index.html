Love and Loss

The Abduction of Persephone

Hades abducts Persephone to be his queen in the underworld. Her mother Demeter's grief causes winter, leading to an agreement where Persephone spends part of each year above and below ground, creating the seasons.

Orpheus and Eurydice

The legendary musician Orpheus descends to the underworld to retrieve his dead wife Eurydice. His music moves even Hades, but he loses her when he looks back too soon.
