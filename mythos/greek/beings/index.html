Supernatural Beings of Greek Myth

Explore the supernatural beings, guardians, and ancient powers that populate Greek mythology - from the mighty Titans to guardian beasts like Cerberus.

Ancient Powers

The Titans

The elder gods who ruled before the Olympians. Children of Gaia and Uranus, they were overthrown by Zeus and his siblings in the Titanomachy.

Typhon

The most fearsome monster in Greek mythology, a serpentine giant with a hundred dragon heads. Father of many monsters including the Chimera, Hydra, and Cerberus.

Cerberus

The three-headed dog who guards the gates of the underworld, preventing the dead from leaving and the living from entering uninvited.
