Dalai Lama - Reincarnation of Avalokiteshvara

The spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism, believed to be the reincarnation of Avalokiteshvara, the Bodhisattva of Compassion. The current (14th) Dalai Lama, Tenzin Gyatso, is a Nobel Peace Prize laureate and global spiritual leader.
