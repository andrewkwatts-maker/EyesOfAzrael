Utu (Shamash)

God of the Sun and Justice

Utu (Shamash in Akkadian) is the Sumerian sun god and god of justice. As the sun, he sees all and serves as the ultimate judge, bringing light and truth to the world.

Attributes

Domains
Sun, justice, truth, law, divination
Symbols
Sun disk, scales of justice, saw
Family
Son of Nanna (moon god), Twin of Inanna
