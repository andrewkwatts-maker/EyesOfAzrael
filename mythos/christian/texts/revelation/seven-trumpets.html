Revelation 8-11: Trumpet Judgments

After the seventh seal, seven angels with trumpets announce escalating judgments echoing the plagues of Egypt. Each trumpet brings partial destruction (1/3 of earth, sea, rivers, light affected), calling humanity to repentance.

The Seven Trumpets

  • 1st Trumpet (8:7): Hail and fire mixed with blood; 1/3 of earth burned
  • 2nd Trumpet (8:8-9): Mountain thrown into sea; 1/3 of sea to blood, 1/3 of ships destroyed
  • 3rd Trumpet (8:10-11): Star "Wormwood" falls; 1/3 of waters bitter
  • 4th Trumpet (8:12): 1/3 of sun, moon, stars darkened
  • 5th Trumpet - 1st Woe (9:1-12): Locusts from abyss torment those without God's seal for 5 months
  • 6th Trumpet - 2nd Woe (9:13-21): Four angels released; 1/3 of humanity killed; people still refuse to repent
  • Interlude (10:1-11:14): Angel with little scroll; Two Witnesses prophesy, killed, resurrected
  • 7th Trumpet - 3rd Woe (11:15-19): "The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of his Messiah"

Exodus Parallels
