The Sealing (Revelation 7:1-8)

  • Four angels hold back four winds from harming earth
  • Angel with seal of living God commands: "Do not harm earth until we seal God's servants"
  • 144,000 sealed from tribes of Israel: Judah, Reuben, Gad, Asher, Naphtali, Manasseh, Simeon, Levi, Issachar, Zebulun, Joseph, Benjamin (12,000 each)
  • Sealed = protected, marked as belonging to God

On Mount Zion (Revelation 14:1-5)

  • 144,000 standing with the Lamb on Mount Zion
  • Father's name written on their foreheads
  • Singing a new song only they can learn
  • "Redeemed from the earth... offered as firstfruits to God and the Lamb"
  • "No lie was found in their mouths; they are blameless"
