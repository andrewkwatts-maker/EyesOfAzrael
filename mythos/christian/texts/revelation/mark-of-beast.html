Revelation 13:16-18

"It also forced all people, great and small, rich and poor, free and slave, to receive a mark on their right hands or on their foreheads, so that they could not buy or sell unless they had the mark, which is the name of the beast or the number of its name. This calls for wisdom. Let the person who has insight calculate the number of the beast, for it is the number of a man. That number is 666."

Key Features

  • Location: Right hand or forehead
  • Function: Required to buy or sell (economic control)
  • Options: Name of beast OR number of its name
  • The Number: 666 (or in some manuscripts, 616)

Interpretations of 666

  • Gematria - Nero Caesar: In Hebrew, ◊†◊®◊ï◊ü ◊ß◊°◊® (Neron Kaisar) = 666. Nero was first major persecutor of Christians.
  • Symbolic - Triple Six: 6 = number of man (created day 6, falls short of 7 = perfection). 666 = humanity exalting itself to godhood, repeatedly falling short.
  • Unholy Trinity: 6-6-6 (dragon, beast, false prophet) vs. divine Trinity
  • Every Tyrant: Not just one individual but the pattern of all anti-God powers throughout history

OT Background

Daniel 3: Nebuchadnezzar's golden image - worship or die. Refusing the mark parallels Shadrach, Meshach, Abednego refusing to bow.

Deuteronomy 6:8: "Bind [God's words] as symbols on your hands and bind them on your foreheads" - the mark of the beast is a demonic counterfeit of God's mark on His people.

Contrast: Mark of Beast vs. Seal of God

  • Beast's Mark (13:16): On hand or forehead, enables buying/selling, leads to judgment
  • God's Seal (7:3, 14:1): On foreheads of 144,000, protects from wrath, Father's name written

Humanity is divided: those who belong to God (sealed) and those who belong to the beast (marked).
