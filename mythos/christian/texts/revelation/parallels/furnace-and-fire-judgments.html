Introduction: Fire as Judgment and Purification

Throughout Scripture, fire serves as the primary symbol of divine judgment, purification, and the consuming presence of God. From the burning bush to the lake of fire, God's holiness is repeatedly depicted as refining flame. The progression from Daniel 3's furnace to Revelation's final judgment reveals a consistent pattern: those who refuse to worship false gods are protected in the fire, while those who bow to idols face ultimate consuming flame.

The Central Parallel: Daniel 3 and Revelation 13-16

Daniel 3 - The Golden Image Revelation 13-16 - The Beast's Image

Daniel 3:1

"King Nebuchadnezzar made an image of gold, sixty cubits high and six cubits wide, and set it up on the plain of Dura in the province of Babylon."

Revelation 13:18

"This calls for wisdom. Let the person who has insight calculate the number of the beast, for it is the number of a man. That number is 666."

The 6-6-6 Connection

Nebuchadnezzar's image is 60 cubits high by 6 cubits wide‚Äîa deliberate pattern of sixes that prefigures the beast's number 666. Both represent human pretension to divine glory (six is the number of man, created on day six, falling short of seven = perfection). The dimensions themselves prophetically point to the ultimate false worship system.

Daniel 3:4-6 Revelation 13:15

Daniel 3:4-6

"Then the herald loudly proclaimed, 'Nations and peoples of every language, this is what you are commanded to do: As soon as you hear the sound... you must fall down and worship the image of gold that King Nebuchadnezzar has set up. Whoever does not fall down and worship will immediately be thrown into a blazing furnace.'"

Revelation 13:15

"The second beast was given power to give breath to the image of the first beast, so that the image could speak and cause all who refused to worship the image to be killed."

Worship on Pain of Death

Both systems enforce universal worship of an image under threat of execution. Daniel 3 = immediate death by furnace; Revelation 13 = death for refusing the mark. The pattern is identical: global empire, enforced idolatry, martyrdom for the faithful.
