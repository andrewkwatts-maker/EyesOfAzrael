1. The Locust Army

Joel 1:6; 2:4-5, 10

"And out of the smoke locusts came down on the earth and were given power like that of scorpions of the earth... The locusts looked like horses prepared for battle. Theological Connection

Joel's locust plague (both literal and symbolic of invading armies) becomes in Revelation a demonic locust army from the abyss, tormenting those without God's seal. Both depict overwhelming divine judgment.
