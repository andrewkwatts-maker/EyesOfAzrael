Revelation 6-8:1: Opening the Scroll

When the Lamb opens each seal on the scroll, judgment unfolds progressively. The first four seals release the Four Horsemen (conquest, war, famine, death). The fifth reveals martyrs crying out for justice. The sixth brings cosmic upheaval. The seventh seal opens to silence before seven trumpet judgments begin.

The Seven Seals

  • 1st Seal (6:1-2): White horse - Conquest
  • 2nd Seal (6:3-4): Red horse - War
  • 3rd Seal (6:5-6): Black horse - Famine
  • 4th Seal (6:7-8): Pale horse - Death (kills 1/4 of earth)
  • 5th Seal (6:9-11): Souls of martyrs under altar crying "How long?"
  • 6th Seal (6:12-17): Great earthquake, sun dark, moon blood, stars fall, "great day of wrath"
  • Interlude (7:1-17): 144,000 sealed from 12 tribes; great multitude in white robes
  • 7th Seal (8:1): Silence in heaven for half an hour, then seven trumpet angels
