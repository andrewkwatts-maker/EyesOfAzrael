Revelation 16: Final Wrath

The seven bowls of God's wrath are the final and most severe judgments. Unlike the partial destruction of trumpets (1/3), the bowls bring complete judgment.

The Seven Bowls

  • 1st Bowl (16:2): Painful sores on those with mark of beast
  • 2nd Bowl (16:3): Sea turns to blood; everything in it dies
  • 3rd Bowl (16:4-7): Rivers and springs turn to blood
  • 4th Bowl (16:8-9): Sun scorches people with fire; they curse God
  • 5th Bowl (16:10-11): Darkness on beast's throne; people gnaw tongues in pain
  • 6th Bowl (16:12-16): Euphrates dried; demons gather kings to Armageddon
  • 7th Bowl (16:17-21): "It is done!" Massive earthquake, cities fall, 100-pound hailstones

Exodus Connection: Bowls echo Egypt's plagues (sores, blood, darkness, hail) but intensified to global scale.
