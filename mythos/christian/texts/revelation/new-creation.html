Key OT Prophecies Fulfilled

  • Isaiah 65:17-25: "See, I will create new heavens and a new earth... no more weeping"
  • Isaiah 25:8: "He will swallow up death forever... wipe away tears"
  • Ezekiel 37:27: "My dwelling place will be with them"
  • Ezekiel 47:1-12: River from temple with healing trees
  • Zechariah 14:7, 11: "There will be continuous day... no more curse"

What is "New"

Revelation 21:5: "He who was seated on the throne said, 'I am making everything new!'"

  • New Heaven and Earth (21:1): Not destroyed but renewed and glorified
  • New Jerusalem (21:2): City from heaven, God's dwelling with humanity
  • New Relationship (21:3): Direct access to God, no mediators needed
  • New Reality (21:4): No death, mourning, crying, or pain
  • New Light (21:23, 22:5): God Himself is the light
  • New Unity (21:24-26): Nations and kings bring glory to God

The Restoration Theme

The New Creation is not merely a return to Eden but an upgrade:

  • Eden was a garden: New Jerusalem is a city-garden (urban + nature)
  • Eden could be lost: New Jerusalem cannot be lost (no more sin or serpent)
  • Eden had one tree: New Jerusalem has a tree on both sides of the river (abundant life)
  • Eden was for two people: New Jerusalem welcomes nations from every tribe and tongue
  • Eden was untested: New Jerusalem is inhabited by the redeemed who overcame
