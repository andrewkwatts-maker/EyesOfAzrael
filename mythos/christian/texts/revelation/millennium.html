Revelation 20: 1000 Years and Great White Throne

Satan Bound for 1000 Years (20:1-3)

  • Angel comes down with key to Abyss and great chain
  • Seizes dragon (Satan) and binds him for 1000 years
  • Thrown into Abyss, locked and sealed
  • Prevented from deceiving the nations during this time
  • After 1000 years, must be released for short time

The Millennial Reign (20:4-6)

  • Thrones given to those who judge
  • Souls of martyrs come to life‚Äîthe first resurrection
  • They reign with Christ for 1000 years
  • "Blessed and holy are those who share in the first resurrection. The second death has no power over them"
  • They will be priests of God and Christ

Satan's Final Rebellion - Gog and Magog (20:7-10)

  • After 1000 years, Satan released from prison
  • Deceives nations (Gog and Magog) to gather for battle
  • They surround the camp of God's people
  • Fire comes down from heaven and devours them
  • Devil thrown into lake of burning sulfur (where beast and false prophet are)
  • Tormented day and night forever and ever

The Great White Throne Judgment (20:11-15)

  • Great white throne; earth and sky flee from God's presence
  • Dead, great and small, stand before the throne
  • Books opened, including the Book of Life
  • Dead judged according to what they had done
  • Death and Hades thrown into lake of fire‚Äîthe second death
  • Anyone whose name not found in Book of Life thrown into lake of fire

Interpretive Views

  • Premillennial: Christ returns, then reigns 1000 literal years
  • Postmillennial: Gospel spreads, 1000 symbolic years of growth, then Christ returns
  • Amillennial: 1000 years symbolic of church age between Christ's ascension and return

All agree on final judgment, Satan's defeat, and Christ's ultimate victory.
