The River of Life and Tree of Life

Revelation 22:1-2 ‚Äî "Then the angel showed me the river of the water of life, as clear as crystal, flowing from the throne of God and of the Lamb down the middle of the great street of the city. On each side of the river stood the tree of life, bearing twelve crops of fruit, yielding its fruit every month. And the leaves of the tree are for the healing of the nations."

Eden Restored and Surpassed

The tree of life from which Adam and Eve were barred (Genesis 3:22-24) is now accessible to all. The river of life echoes both Eden's river (Gen 2:10) and Ezekiel's temple river (Ezek 47:1-12). Paradise is restored, but now in an urban garden‚Äîa city infused with Edenic life.

Healing of the nations: The leaves of the tree bring healing, suggesting ongoing restoration and flourishing. The nations that once raged against God (Ps 2:1) are now healed and walk in His light (Rev 21:24).

Old Testament Fulfillments

New Heavens and New Earth (Isaiah 65:17-25)

Isaiah prophesied: "See, I will create new heavens and a new earth. The former things will not be remembered... the sound of weeping and of crying will be heard in it no more." Revelation 21 is the ultimate fulfillment.

God Dwells with His People (Ezekiel 37:27; 48:35)

Ezekiel's vision concluded: "My dwelling place will be with them; I will be their God, and they will be my people" and the city's name: "THE LORD IS THERE" (Ezek 48:35). This promise reaches its zenith in Rev 21:3.

No More Tears (Isaiah 25:8)

Isaiah promised: "He will swallow up death forever. The Sovereign LORD will wipe away the tears from all faces." Fulfilled in Rev 21:4.
