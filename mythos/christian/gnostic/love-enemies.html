Historical Examples: Enemy Love Embodied

As Stephen was being stoned to death, he cried out: "Lord, do not hold this sin against them" (Acts 7:60). In his final moments, he prayed for his murderers‚Äîembodying Christ's teaching perfectly. After surviving Nazi concentration camps where her sister died, Corrie ten Boom encountered one of her former guards at a church meeting. He extended his hand, asking for forgiveness.
