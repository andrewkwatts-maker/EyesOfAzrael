Apostles, Church Fathers, Martyrs, and Mystics

The heroes and saints of Christianity - men and women who shaped Christian history and spirituality through their faith, sacrifice, and extraordinary lives.

📖 Old Testament Prophets & Patriarchs

⚖️

Moses

The great lawgiver and prophet who led the Israelites out of Egypt and received the Ten Commandments from God.

🔥

Elijah

The fiery prophet who challenged the prophets of Baal, was fed by ravens, and ascended to heaven in a chariot of fire.

🦁

Daniel

The prophet of the Babylonian Exile, interpreter of dreams, survivor of the lions' den, and recipient of apocalyptic visions.

⚔️ The Apostles

🗝️

Saint Peter

The Rock upon which Christ built His Church, first Pope, keeper of the keys, who denied Christ three times yet was restored to lead the apostles.

📖

Saint John

The Beloved Disciple, author of the Fourth Gospel and Revelation, theologian of divine love, the only apostle not martyred.
