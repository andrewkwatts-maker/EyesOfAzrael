Visual Language of Faith

Christian symbolism weaves a rich visual tapestry that communicates divine mysteries, theological truths, and sacred narratives. From the cross of Christ's victory over death to ancient cryptograms that identified believers in persecution, these sacred signs connect the visible to the invisible, the earthly to the eternal.
