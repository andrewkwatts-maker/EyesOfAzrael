### 2. Geographical Metadata
- **originPoint**: Precise or regional coordinates within the Nile Valley

- **culturalArea**: "Ancient Near East"

### 3. Temporal Metadata
- **firstAttestation**: Earliest archaeological or literary references

  - Confidence level (certain, probable, possible, speculative)
- **historicalDate**: Full date ranges from origin to end
- **timelinePosition**: Dynastic period classification
  - Old Kingdom (2686-2181 BCE)
  - Middle Kingdom (2055-1650 BCE)
  - New Kingdom (1550-1077 BCE)
- **culturalPeriod**: Comprehensive period descriptions
- **peakPopularity**: Periods of maximum cultural significance
