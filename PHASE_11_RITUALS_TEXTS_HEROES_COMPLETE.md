## üé® Technical Implementation

### Spinner.css Integration Patterns

**Standard Depth (3 levels):**
```html
<!-- mythos/greek/rituals/offerings.html -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="../../../css/spinner.css">
```

**Deep Nesting (4 levels):**
```html
<!-- mythos/christian/heroes/disciples/peter.html -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="../../../../css/spinner.css">

<!-- mythos/christian/gnostic/texts/index.html -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="../../../../css/spinner.css">
```

**Placement Strategy:**
1. After `mythology-colors.css` (when present)
2. After `theme-base.css` (when mythology-colors.css absent)
3. Before corpus-links.css or smart-links.css
