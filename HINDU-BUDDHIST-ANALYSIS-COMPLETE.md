## Key Insights Provided

### Hindu Mythology Evolution
- **Brahma**: Conceptually important (Vedic) but personally minor (few temples)
- **Shiva**: Transformed from fierce (Vedic Rudra) to supreme consciousness (tantric peak)
- **Vishnu**: Elevated from minor solar deity to supreme lord with theology
- **Krishna**: Evolved from regional hero to cosmic being embodying divine love
- **Pattern**: Ritual → Philosophy → Narrative → Sectarian → Tantric → Global

### Buddhist Mythology Evolution
- **Buddha**: Changed from exemplary human (Early) to cosmic principle (Mahayana)
- **Bodhisattvas**: Emerged as compassionate helpers (contrast to individual focus)
- **Avalokiteshvara**: Shows cultural adaptation (male→female across regions)
- **Regional Variation**: Different schools emphasize different aspects
- **Tantric Integration**: Combined esoteric practice with philosophical Buddhism

### Universal Patterns
- Philosophical elevation (practical → principle)
- Sectarian development (unified → diverse schools)
- Geographic expansion (local → global)
- Gender flexibility (manifestations as masculine, feminine, both)
- Textual accumulation (layers of meaning through periods)
- Living traditions (active practices, not historical relics)
