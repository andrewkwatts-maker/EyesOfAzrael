Creation Myths

Stories of cosmic origins, the birth of the universe, gods, and humanity. These narratives explore the fundamental question of existence and order emerging from chaos.

Explore Creation Myths

Flood/Deluge Myths

Universal narratives of catastrophic floods sent to cleanse or reset creation. Found across cultures, these stories often feature divine judgment, survival, and renewal.

Explore Flood Myths

Dying and Rising God

Deities who experience death and resurrection, symbolizing seasonal cycles, agricultural renewal, and the promise of rebirth and transformation.

Explore Dying and Rising God

Hero's Journey

The monomyth of the hero who ventures forth, faces trials, achieves victory, and returns transformed. The quintessential pattern of personal transformation and adventure.

Explore Hero's Journey

Divine Combat Myth

Epic battles between gods and primordial forces of chaos. These narratives establish cosmic order through conflict and the triumph of civilization over chaos.

Explore Divine Combat

Fall from Grace

Stories of paradise lost, the transition from an ideal state to a flawed existence. These narratives explore the origins of suffering, mortality, and human limitation.

Explore Fall from Grace
