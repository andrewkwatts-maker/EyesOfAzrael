The Sacred Mountain

Home ‚Üí Archetypes ‚Üí Place Archetypes ‚Üí Sacred Mountain

The Sacred Mountain represents the archetypal vertical axis connecting earth to heaven, the terrestrial to the celestial. Across world mythologies, mountains serve as the dwelling places of gods, sites of divine revelation, and pathways for human ascent toward the divine. Rising from the foundation of earth to pierce the sky, the Sacred Mountain embodies spiritual elevation, cosmic order, and the meeting point between mortal and immortal realms.
