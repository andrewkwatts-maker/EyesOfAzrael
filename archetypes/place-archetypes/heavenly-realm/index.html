The Heavenly Realm represents the archetypal upper world of light, divine order, and transcendent perfection. As the dwelling place of gods, angels, and celestial beings, heaven embodies the highest aspirations of consciousness, spiritual law, and cosmic harmony. Characterized by radiant light, eternal bliss, and perfect order, this celestial domain stands as the ultimate destination for righteous souls and the source of divine wisdom, justice, and providence that govern the lower worlds.
