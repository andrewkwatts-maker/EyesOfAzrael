The World Tree

Home → Archetypes → Place Archetypes → World Tree

The World Tree represents the archetypal cosmic axis that connects all realms of existence. With roots reaching deep into the underworld, trunk anchored in the middle world of mortals, and branches extending to the heavens, the World Tree embodies the vertical structure of the cosmos. This living axis mundi serves as a pathway between worlds, a source of cosmic nourishment, and a symbol of the interconnectedness of all life and reality.
