Place Archetypes

Home → Archetypes → Place Archetypes

Place archetypes represent the universal locations that structure mythological cosmology across world traditions. These sacred spaces transcend individual cultures, appearing in diverse forms yet maintaining core symbolic meanings. From the depths of the underworld to the heights of heaven, from the center of the world tree to the edges of primordial chaos, these archetypal places map the spiritual geography of human consciousness and cosmic order.

Underworld

Afterlife & Shadow Realm

The realm of the dead and shadow, where souls journey after death. A place of transformation, testing, and sometimes return. Dark, mysterious, and often guarded by fearsome beings.

Explore the Underworld

Sacred Mountain

Cosmic Axis & Divine Dwelling

The meeting place of earth and sky, where mortals can approach the divine. A vertical axis connecting the mundane world to higher realms, often the dwelling place of gods.

Ascend the Sacred Mountain

World Tree

Axis Mundi & Cosmic Connection

The cosmic tree that connects all realms of existence, its roots in the underworld, trunk in the middle world, and branches reaching to heaven. The axis around which the cosmos revolves.

Climb the World Tree

Paradise

Golden Age & Lost Eden

The perfect realm of harmony and abundance, often located in the distant past or hidden present. A place of immortality, peace, and unity with nature and the divine.

Enter Paradise

Primordial Waters

Chaos & Creation Potential

The formless waters of chaos that existed before creation. The deep, dark abyss from which all order emerges and to which all may return. The source of both creation and destruction.

Dive into Primordial Waters

Heavenly Realm

Divine Abode & Celestial Order

The upper realm of light, order, and divine perfection. Home to gods, angels, and celestial beings. A place of eternal light, cosmic law, and transcendent beauty.

Ascend to Heaven
