Primordial Waters

Home ‚Üí Archetypes ‚Üí Place Archetypes ‚Üí Primordial Waters

The Primordial Waters represent the archetypal formless chaos that existed before creation. This deep, dark abyss embodies the undifferentiated state preceding cosmic order, containing infinite potential for both creation and destruction. Neither fully void nor fully substance, these waters symbolize the boundary between non-existence and existence, the source from which all ordered reality emerges and to which all may return. In their depths lies both terror and possibility.
