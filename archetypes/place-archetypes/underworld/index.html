The Underworld

Home ‚Üí Archetypes ‚Üí Place Archetypes ‚Üí Underworld

The Underworld represents the archetypal realm of the dead, darkness, and shadow. Across world mythologies, this subterranean or hidden domain serves as the destination for souls after death, a place of transformation, judgment, and sometimes return. Often guarded by fearsome beings and separated from the living world by rivers, gates, or other barriers, the Underworld embodies humanity's deepest fears and mysteries about death, the afterlife, and what lies beyond mortal existence.
