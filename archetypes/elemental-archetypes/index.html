Cross-Cultural Patterns

While individual traditions may emphasize different elements, these archetypal forces appear universally:

  • Greek: Ouranos (sky), Gaia (earth), Pontos (sea), Tartaros (abyss), Erebos (darkness)
  • Norse: Muspelheim (fire), Niflheim (ice/mist), Midgard (earth), Ginnungagap (void)
  • Hindu: Agni (fire), Vayu (wind), Prithvi (earth), Varuna (waters), Akasha (ether)
  • Chinese: Wu Xing - Fire, Earth, Metal, Water, Wood
  • Egyptian: Atum (primordial), Shu (air), Tefnut (moisture), Geb (earth), Nut (sky)
