üìä Our Analytical Approach

Each analysis in this section follows a rigorous methodology designed to present information objectively while acknowledging the inherent uncertainties in comparative mythology:

  • Multiple Perspectives: We present mainstream academic views alongside alternative theories, clearly labeling each.
  • Evidence Quality Assessment: Sources are rated based on archaeological support, scholarly consensus, and primary text availability.
  • Confidence Levels: Each claim includes a confidence rating based on the strength of supporting evidence.
  • Cross-References: Extensive links to original mythology pages for deeper exploration.
  • Transparent Limitations: We acknowledge what we don't know and where evidence is contested.
