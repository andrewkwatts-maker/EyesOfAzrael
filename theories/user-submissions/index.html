Submission Guidelines

  • Evidence-Based Reasoning Support your theory with specific textual references, archaeological findings, or scholarly sources. Speculation should be clearly labeled as such.
  • Respect Multiple Perspectives Acknowledge alternative interpretations and mainstream academic views. Present your theory as one possible interpretation, not absolute truth.
  • Clear Attribution Give credit to other researchers whose work you build upon. Plagiarism or uncredited borrowing of ideas will not be accepted.
  • Cultural Sensitivity Treat all mythological traditions with respect. Avoid dismissive language about any culture's beliefs or practices.
  • Constructive Discourse Engage thoughtfully with other theories and submissions. Criticism should be constructive and focused on ideas, not individuals.
  • Original Content Submissions should represent your own analysis and synthesis. Direct quotes should be properly cited and comprise a minority of your submission.
