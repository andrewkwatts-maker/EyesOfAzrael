ü™ê The Planetary Hypothesis (Zecharia Sitchin)

Proponent: Zecharia Sitchin (1920-2010)

Key Works: The 12th Planet (1976), The Cosmic Code

Core Claims:

  • Tiamat = Planet: The goddess Tiamat represents a water planet that once existed between Mars and Jupiter
  • Marduk = Nibiru: The god Marduk represents a rogue planet (Nibiru) that entered our solar system
  • The Collision: Nibiru's moons struck Tiamat, creating Earth from one half and the asteroid belt from the other
  • Mars Connection: Mars (Lahmu) may have orbited Tiamat as a moon before the catastrophe
  • Our Moon: Earth's Moon was Kingu, Tiamat's satellite, captured in the collision
  • Origin of Life: Life may have originated on the water world Tiamat and transferred to Earth through the debris

‚öîÔ∏è The Cosmic War Hypothesis (Dr. Joseph P. Farrell)

Key Work: The Cosmic War: Interplanetary Warfare, Modern Physics, and Ancient Texts (2007)

Featured Discussion: Forum Borealis Podcast - "Ancient War in Heaven" (3-part series)

Core Claims:

  • Deliberate Warfare: The destruction of Tiamat was not a natural collision but deliberate warfare by an ancient advanced civilization
  • Scalar Weapons: The "divine weapons" described in myths may reference scalar electromagnetic technology capable of disrupting matter at planetary scale
  • Timeline: The events occurred approximately 3.2 million years ago, based on Van Flandern's research
  • Mars as Casualty: The scarring of Mars (Valles Marineris, hemispheric dichotomy) resulted from the same conflict
  • Pyramids Connection: Ancient monuments may be remnants or replicas of the weapons technology
  • Tablets of Destiny: This artifact may represent control technology for devastating weapons systems

üî≠ The Exploded Planet Hypothesis

Proponent: Dr. Tom Van Flandern (US Naval Observatory)

Scientific Basis: Astronomical observation and orbital mechanics

Supporting Evidence Cited:

  • Titius-Bode Law: The mathematical sequence predicting planetary distances indicates a "missing planet" where the asteroid belt exists
  • Asteroid Differentiation: Some asteroids show evidence of having been part of a larger body with core, mantle, and crust
  • Mars Dichotomy: The dramatic difference between Mars's northern and southern hemispheres suggests catastrophic bombardment
  • Comet Origins: The orbital characteristics of comets suggest origins from an exploded planet rather than the Oort cloud

üìÖ WAR TIMELINE (Alternative Chronology)

Based on Sitchin's interpretation of Sumerian texts and Van Flandern's astronomical research

PHASE 1: Primordial Unity

Timeframe: ~4.5 billion years ago (solar system formation)

Tiamat (water planet) and Apsu (possibly the Sun or proto-Sun) exist in primordial state. First planetary bodies (the "gods") form from their cosmic union.

PHASE 2: First Conflict - Death of Apsu

Timeframe: Uncertain (early solar system)

Ea uses "magic" (advanced technology or gravitational manipulation) to neutralize Apsu. In planetary terms, this may represent the stabilization of the inner solar system or the "capture" of certain orbital bodies.

Casualty: Apsu "slain" - rendered dormant/captured

PHASE 3: Tiamat's Rage & Army Assembly

Timeframe: Prior to ~3.2 million years ago

Tiamat creates/summons the Eleven Monsters (possibly captured moons or orbital debris). Elevates Kingu as champion and grants him the Tablet of Destinies (control technology?).

Tiamat's Declaration: "I have cast the spell for you, I have made you great in the assembly of the gods!"

PHASE 4: Approach of Marduk/Nibiru

Timeframe: ~3.2 million years ago

Nibiru enters the inner solar system on a clockwise (retrograde) orbit. The younger gods rally behind Marduk after Anu and Ea fail to challenge Tiamat.

Marduk's Demand: "If I am to be your champion, make my destiny supreme!"

PHASE 5: First Pass - The Shattering

Timeframe: ~3.2 million years ago

Nibiru's moons strike Tiamat. One satellite ("North Wind") impacts directly, "splitting her heart."

Weapons Deployed: Four winds, Evil Wind, Hurricane, Tempest, Whirlwind, Matchless Wind, Fourfold Wind, Sevenfold Wind

Casualties:

  • Tiamat - mortally wounded, immobilized
  • Multiple monster satellites destroyed or scattered

PHASE 6: Second Pass - The Division

Timeframe: Shortly after first pass

Marduk/Nibiru returns, splitting Tiamat's corpse "like a fish for drying."

Results:

  • Upper Half: Becomes the "hammered bracelet" (asteroid belt)
  • Lower Half: Pushed into new orbit, becomes Earth
  • Kingu: Captured, becomes Earth's Moon
  • Tiamat's Waters: Transferred to new Earth (oceans)

PHASE 7: New Order Established

Timeframe: Post-collision

Marduk establishes new celestial order. Creates stations for the gods (planetary orbits), establishes the calendar, and uses Kingu's "blood" (possibly genetic material or water) in the creation of humanity.

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Academic Perspective

These alternative theories are not accepted by mainstream scholarship. Professional Assyriologists, astronomers, and historians have raised significant objections:

  • Translation Issues: Sitchin's translations of Sumerian/Akkadian have been disputed by professional linguists
  • Anachronistic Reading: Critics argue these theories project modern astronomical concepts onto texts intended as theology/literature
  • Accretion Model: Mainstream astronomy holds the asteroid belt never formed into a planet due to Jupiter's gravitational influence
  • Mythological Function: The Enuma Elish can be understood as political propaganda legitimizing Babylonian supremacy
  • No Physical Evidence: No archaeological or geological evidence definitively supports these interpretations

These theories are presented for intellectual exploration, not as established fact. They represent alternative frameworks for examining mythology alongside traditional scholarly, psychological, and comparative approaches.
