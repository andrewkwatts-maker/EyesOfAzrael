The Sermon on the Mount

Matthew 5-7: Jesus as the New Moses, delivering the manifesto of God's Kingdom from the mountain. The greatest sermon ever preached - revealing both the impossible standard of righteousness and pointing to Christ himself as the only fulfillment.

Matthew 5:1-2 - "Now when Jesus saw the crowds, he went up on a mountainside and sat down. His disciples came to him, and he began to teach them."

â›°ï¸ Jesus as the New Moses

Just as Moses ascended Mount Sinai to receive the Law (Exodus 19-20), Jesus ascends the mountain to deliver the new covenant. Where Moses gave the Ten Commandments inscribed on stone tablets, Jesus gives the Beatitudes and kingdom ethics written on the heart. The Sermon on the Mount is not just moral teaching - it is the authoritative word of God incarnate, fulfilling and transcending the old law.
