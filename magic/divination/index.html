Methods of Seeking Knowledge

Ancient techniques for perceiving patterns, uncovering hidden truths, and glimpsing possible futures through symbolic systems.

🃏

Tarot

78-card symbolic system combining Kabbalistic, alchemical, and archetypal imagery for divination and spiritual insight.

☯

I Ching

Ancient Chinese hexagram system using 64 combinations to divine cosmic patterns and guide decision-making.

ᚅ

Runes

Nordic alphabet system imbued with magical power, used for divination, spellcasting, and communication with the divine.

♈

Astrology

Study of celestial bodies' influence on earthly events through zodiacal and planetary systems.

🦴

Oracle Bones

Ancient Chinese practice of inscribing questions on bones or shells and interpreting the resulting cracks.

⛰

Geomancy

Divination by interpreting markings in earth, sand, or stones; includes Feng Shui and Arabic sand divination.
