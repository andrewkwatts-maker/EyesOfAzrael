   - Imperial court mythology
   - Divine descent (Tenshin) establishment

   - 1,200-year integration period
   - Theological framework: Honji suijaku
   - Peak during Heian period (794-1185 CE)
   - All deities have Buddhist equivalents documented

4. Medieval Militarization (11th-16th centuries)
   - Samurai adoption of mythology
   - Hachiman as war god
   - Religious innovation for military class

5. Edo Period Formalization (1603-1868)
   - Shrine hierarchy standardization
   - Tokugawa shogunate patronage
   - Stable religious coexistence

6. State Shinto Era (1868-1945)
   - Imperial nationalism ideology
   - Forced Buddhist separation
   - Amaterasu as national symbol
   - Military sacralization

7. Post-War Revival (1945-present)
   - Religious freedom restoration
   - Continued folk worship vitality
   - Cultural tradition preservation
