- Intentional action shapes future
- Types: mental, verbal, physical
- Quality: wholesome, unwholesome, neutral
- Determines rebirth realm

- Extinction of greed, hatred, delusion
- Not annihilation, not heaven
- Beyond concepts and words
- Sopadhishesa (with remainder) vs. Nirupadhishesa

**Buddhist Klesha:**
- Three poisons: greed, hatred, delusion
- Root causes of suffering
- Bind beings to samsara
- Overcome through practice

**Persian Asha:**
- Truth, order, righteousness
- Cosmic principle and ethical ideal
- Embodied by Ahura Mazda
- Fire represents Asha

**Persian Druj:**
- Lie, chaos, disorder
- Opposite of Asha
- Embodied by Angra Mainyu
- Causes pollution and death

**Christian Grace:**
- Unmerited favor from God
- Types: common, saving, sanctifying, actual
- Received through faith
- Enables transformation

**Christian Trinity:**
- One God in three persons
- Father, Son, Holy Spirit
- Unity of essence, distinction of persons
- Central mystery of Christianity

**Sumerian Me:**
- Divine powers and decrees
- Foundation of civilization
- Over 100 different me
- Can be transferred (Inanna's theft)

**Egyptian Ma'at:**
- Truth, justice, order, balance
- Cosmic and ethical principle
- Pharaoh upholds Ma'at
- Heart weighed against Ma'at's feather
