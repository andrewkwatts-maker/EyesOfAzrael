## Quick Integration

### Step 1: Add Scripts to Page
```html
<script src="/js/deity-domain-icons.js"></script>
<script src="/js/entity-renderer-domain-icons.js"></script>
```

### Step 2: Use in Firebase Data
```json
{
  "domains": ["war", "sky", "justice"]
}
```

Icons automatically appear! No additional configuration needed.

## Common Domain Combinations by Mythology

### Greek
- **Zeus**: sky, thunder, justice
- **Athena**: wisdom, war
- **Ares**: war, battle
- **Aphrodite**: love, beauty
- **Hades**: death, underworld
- **Poseidon**: sea, water
- **Apollo**: sun, light, healing
- **Artemis**: moon, nature
- **Hermes**: trickster
- **Hephaestus**: fire, forge

### Egyptian
- **Ra**: sun, creator
- **Osiris**: death, fertility
- **Isis**: wisdom, healing
- **Anubis**: death
- **Thoth**: wisdom, moon
- **Hathor**: love, beauty
- **Ptah**: creator, crafts
- **Sekhmet**: war, healing

### Norse
- **Odin**: wisdom, war, death
- **Thor**: sky, thunder, war
- **Freya**: love, fertility, war
- **Loki**: trickster, chaos
- **Hel**: death, underworld
- **Tyr**: war, justice
- **Frigg**: wisdom

### Hindu
- **Brahma**: creator
- **Vishnu**: creator
- **Shiva**: death, creator
- **Saraswati**: wisdom, learning
- **Lakshmi**: prosperity, fertility
- **Indra**: sky, thunder, war
- **Yama**: death, justice
- **Agni**: fire

### Other Mythologies
