## DENOMINATIONAL DIFFERENCES

### Catholic
- Recognizes 7 archangels (Michael, Gabriel, Raphael + 4 from Enoch tradition)
- Accepts Tobit (deuterocanonical)
- Full nine-choir hierarchy
- Guardian angel feast: October 2
- Michaelmas: September 29

### Orthodox
- Accepts all 7 archangels including apocryphal
- Honors Uriel, Raguel, Remiel, Sariel
- Full hierarchy
- Rich iconographic tradition
- November 8: Synaxis of the Archangels

### Protestant
- Focus on biblical angels only: Michael, Gabriel
- Raphael sometimes (Anglicans accept Tobit)
- Reject apocryphal archangels (Uriel, Raguel, Remiel, Sariel)
- Nine choirs acknowledged but less emphasized
- More biblical, less systematized
