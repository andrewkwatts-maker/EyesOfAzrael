# Eyes of Azrael: Conspiracy Theory Content Plan

## Overview
A comprehensive plan to add 5,000+ conspiracy-related assets to the mythology encyclopedia, exploring the intersection of mythology, secret societies, alternative history, and fringe theories.

## Content Categories Structure

### New Entity Types Needed
1. **conspiracies** - Main conspiracy theory entries
2. **secret_societies** - Organizations and groups
3. **hidden_history** - Alternative historical narratives
4. **cover_ups** - Alleged suppressed information
5. **figures** - Key personalities and researchers
6. **artifacts_mysterious** - Disputed or legendary objects
7. **locations_mysterious** - Places with paranormal/conspiracy significance
8. **events_unexplained** - Unexplained phenomena and incidents
9. **technologies_suppressed** - Alleged hidden technologies
10. **connections** - Cross-mythology conspiracy connections

## Quality Standards

### Each Entry Must Include:
1. **Description**: 500+ character scholarly overview
2. **Sources**: At least 3 sources (books, researchers, primary texts)
3. **Connections**: Minimum 3 links to related assets
4. **Evidence/Claims**: Documented claims with sources
5. **Debunking/Criticism**: Balanced perspective where applicable
6. **Related Mythology**: Connections to established mythology
7. **Key Figures**: Associated researchers or historical figures
8. **Timeline**: Historical context and dating
9. **Imagery**: Symbolic associations and visual descriptions
10. **Search Terms**: Variants and aliases for discovery

### Cross-Linking Requirements:
- Every conspiracy links to relevant mythology
- Every mythology asset updated with conspiracy connections
- Network visualization data for relationship mapping
